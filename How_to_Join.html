How to Join

We hold try-outs in the fall, usually around 2 weeks into the semester. Come find our booth at Student org night to meet us and get more information about our club and try-out dates. Try-outs take place over two weeks, then emails will be sent out to those who are offered spots on the team.

Other Information

Time Commitment: We practice four days a week with each practice lasting two hours. Practices are required, unless there is a conflict with class related activities. We do have practice players who are not required to attend every practice, but they do not attend tournaments.

Dues: Dues are typically $50 in the fall since we attend less tournaments and $90 in the spring. This may vary.
