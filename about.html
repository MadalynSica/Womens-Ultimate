About us

Ever heard of ultimate frisbee? We are an all women's team with about 20 members and love to throw around a plastic disc. Our club competes in USA Ultimate (USAU) tournaments. We practice and compete year round. Fall is the developmental season and spring is our competitive season. In the fall we compete in about three tournaments and in the spring we compete in about four to five tournaments. We are a fun team on and off the field! We hold try-outs only in the fall semester!
